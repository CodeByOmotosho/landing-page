Our Focus

At HealthBridge, we are committed to making the delivery of care simpler and more efficient by leveraging technology, especially artificial intelligence.

Our flagship product, CareFlo, optimises risk stratification for patients, matches them to best life-saving options through referrals and recommendations, and facilitates immediate access to emergent care.

It saves time in assessing and treating critically ill patients, eliminates information handicap on the part of patients, reduces factors that contribute to physician burnout, and promotes patient-centric care. Also, it equips patients with technological tools that empower them to be actively involved in their own care.

Our Vision

To be at the forefront of innovations that promote ease of healthcare delivery in Nigeria and Africa.

Our Mission

Driving these innovations via AI-powered technology and creating strategic partnerships that streamline healthcare access and delivery.

Our Values

Trust, excellence, and companionship, grounded in our dedication to making healthcare a common good for both the patient and the physician.
